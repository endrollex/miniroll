[b]minicode - easy editing XHTML[/b]

[color]green]Introduction:[/color]

minicode is a lightweight markup language for [url]https://github.com/endrollex/miniroll[miniroll[/url] easy editing, like BBCode.
The available tags are usually indicated by square brackets ([ ]) surrounding a keyword.
Mechanism of minicode is base on "Search and Replace", the process is reversible and very simply.
It abbreviated commonly-used XHTML to short code, or mixes others XHTML.
The result is a HyperText of XHTML tags, some with a little comment markup (<!--...-->) to reversion.

[color]green]Syntax:[/color]
[html]<ol><li>The minicode cannot nest:
<pre style="background-color: #C0C0C0;">[url]http://goo.gl/[/url] OK
[b][url]http://goo.gl/[/url][/b] This expression is ERROR!!!
</pre>Because nest will be complex and bloated.
In this situation, you can use XHTML directly.</li></ol>[/html][color]green]Keywords and Usage:[/color]
